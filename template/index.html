Acerca de AireDellin

Airedellín is a citizen science initiative aimed at addressing the growing issue of air quality in Medellín. We leverage modern technologies like machine learning, interactive maps, and sensor data to provide insights and predictions on PM2.5 levels.

Our goal is to empower the community to take actionable steps toward improving public health.
