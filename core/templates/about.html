Acerca de AireDellin

Airedellín is a citizen science initiative aimed at addressing the growing issue of air quality in Medellín. We leverage modern technologies like machine learning, interactive maps, and sensor data to provide insights and predictions on PM2.5 levels.

Our goal is to empower the community to take actionable steps toward improving public health.

Map Visualization

Real-time, interactive maps displaying air quality data from various sensors across the city, color-coded to show PM2.5 levels.

Predictions

Use machine learning models like Linear Regression, LSTM, and XGBoost to predict air quality patterns based on historical data.

Heatmap

Visualize the intensity of PM2.5 concentrations with a heatmap to understand the air quality variations in Medellín.
